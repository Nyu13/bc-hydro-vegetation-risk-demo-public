# BC Hydro Internal Data Needed for Formal PoC

## Must-Have

- Internal outage history (event-level, time-stamped, cause, restoration)
- Feeder/circuit/corridor topology and electrical network model
- GIS asset data (lines, poles, devices, substations, conductor metadata)
- Weather observations and forecast archives aligned to service territory

## Strongly Recommended

- Vegetation inspection and patrol records
- Vegetation treatment history and treatment recency
- Right-of-way (ROW) / corridor maintenance records
- Restoration and crew response logs

## Recommended Enrichment

- Asset condition and maintenance backlog indicators
- LiDAR and high-resolution satellite observations
- Terrain, slope, and access constraints
- SCADA / ADMS / protection event streams
- Work management and switching records
- Customer impact and critical-load segmentation
- Climate and fire-weather indicators (drought, wind event severity context)

## Why This Matters

Public/proxy data can illustrate risk concepts, but operationally credible predictions require internal BC Hydro datasets for calibration, validation, and operational trust.
